Gamp-Gamp's Lemon Cheesecake
  • 12 cream cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 lemon juice
  • 1 lemon rind
  • 2 vanilla essence
  • 14 digestives
  • 1.5 melted butter
  • 6 sugar
  • 10 sour cream
  • 1.5 sugar
  • 1 vanilla essence
    •
  • Heat oven to 180.
  • Crush the digestives, add the melted butter and mix. Press firmly onto the base and sides of a tin to make the base.
  • Put base in oven for 5 minutes.
  • Beat the eggs, then add the cream cheese, lemon juice and rind, 2 vanilla, and 6 sugar.
  • Pour mixture onto the base.
  • Bake for 30 minutes (until the mixture is set), then cool for 5 minutes.
  • Mix sour cream, 1.5 sugar, and 1 vanilla, then pour on top
  • Bake for 10 minutes, then turn the oven off and leave the cheesecake to cool in the over overnight.
