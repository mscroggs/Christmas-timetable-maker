main vegan Christmas Butternut Wreath
  • 1 pack ready rolled puff pastry
  • 6-10 slices of bread
  • 60 margarine
  • 1 onion
  • 3 dried sage
  • 1 butternut squash
  • 1 vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
    •
  • Chop the butternut squash into 5 to 1 cubes.
  • Stir squash cubes in oil and add a little salt and pepper, then roast at 200 for around 45 minutes until soft.
  • Blend the bread to make breadcrumbs.
  • Add the chopped onion, sage, margarine and some pepper to the breadcrumbs and blend to make stuffing.
  • Form the stuffing into around 6 stuffing balls.
  • Unroll the pastry, cut in half lengthways and arrange into a very long rectangle.
  • Place the stuffing balls evenly spaced along the pastry. Put spoonfuls of butternut squash cubes between the stuffing balls.
  • Pinch the pastry together wherever there is a gap between stuffing and squash.
  • Bend the pastry around to make a wreath shape.
  • Bake in the oven at 200 for around 20 minutes until the pastry is golden.
